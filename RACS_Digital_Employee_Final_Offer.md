# Digital Employee × RACS: System Offering

## Executive Summary

Digital Employee provides a website plus four AI-powered automation modules that handle repetitive business development tasks (lead capture, regulatory monitoring, content publishing, prospect research). This allows RACS to scale lead generation and client engagement without proportional headcount growth. Modules are independent and can be implemented sequentially.

---

## System Modules & Specifications

### Module 1: Compliance Search Assistant

**Capability:**
- Website chatbot answering RACS service-related compliance questions (HS Search tool as sales funnel)
- Covers: Saudi SASO/SABER, UAE ECAS/EQM, Halal, SFDA, G-Mark
- Lead capture: collects name, email, phone before access
- Search database: 50 regulations per query, auto-paginated for cost control
- Lead submission: structured data (name, phone, product)

**Technical Specs:**
- Response time: <2 seconds (p95)
- Uptime: 99.5%
- Concurrent users: 100+
- Capacity: up to 3,000 conversations/month

**Setup: $18,000 | Annual Retainer: $1,800**

- Data verification: 90%+ accuracy

**Technical Specs:**
- Delivery: 9:00 AM Saudi time daily
- Coverage: ~250+ leads/month identified
- Data refresh: LinkedIn profiles weekly
